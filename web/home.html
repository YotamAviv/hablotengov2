HabloTengo

Share contact info — trusted, secure, nothing published or public.

Leverages our decentralized identity network, see one-of-us.net.

This is a demo with pre-loaded Simpsons contacts. You can sign out and sign in as someone else to see their view, or change settings — though other visitors may be doing the same.

HabloTengo te deja share tu contact info — teléfono, email, dirección — solo con la gente que tú trust, usando la red ONE-OF-US.NET. A diferencia del Nerdster, que es open y transparent by design, HabloTengo keeps it private: nada se publica, y solo tu trusted network puede ver tu card.
HabloTengo lets you share contact info — phone, email, address — only with people you trust, through the ONE-OF-US.NET identity network. Unlike the Nerdster, which is open and fully transparent by design, HabloTengo keeps everything private: nothing is published or public, and only your trusted contacts can see your card.
